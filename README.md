# Turkey 2026 — Hotel Selection

Interactive page with 20 curated 5-star Ultra All Inclusive hotels in Turkey for a family vacation.

**Live:** [turkey26.vercel.app](https://turkey26.vercel.app)

## Parameters

- **Dates:** July 15 — August 15, 2026 (8–10 nights)
- **Guests:** 2 adults + 1 child (14 years)
- **Hotels:** 5-star, Ultra All Inclusive, beachfront (1st line)
- **Prices:** CHF, calibrated from Booking.com

## Regions

| Region | Hotels | Price range (CHF) |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Belek | 12 | 7 500 – 15 500 |
| Side | 3 | 7 000 – 9 000 |
| Kemer | 3 | 6 000 – 10 000 |
| Lara (Antalya) | 2 | 8 000 – 10 500 |

## Features

- Sort by price or rating
- Clickable cards → Booking.com with pre-filled dates & guests
- Real hotel photos from official websites
- Responsive design (mobile + desktop)

## Stack

Single `index.html` — no dependencies, no build step.
