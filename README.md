# Turkey 2026 — Budget Hotel Selection

Interactive page with 20 budget-friendly All Inclusive hotels in Turkey + flights from Zurich for a family vacation.

**Live:** [turkey26.vercel.app](https://turkey26.vercel.app)

## Parameters

- **Dates:** July 15 — August 15, 2026 (10 nights)
- **Guests:** 2 adults + 1 child (14 years)
- **Hotels:** 4-5 star, All Inclusive / Ultra AI, beachfront
- **Flights:** Zurich (ZRH) → Antalya (AYT), round-trip, 3 passengers
- **Prices:** CHF, total (hotel + flights ~1 000 CHF via SunExpress)

## Regions

| Region | Hotels | Total price range (CHF) |
|--------|--------|-------------------------|
| Alanya | 4 | 3 500 – 4 500 |
| Kemer | 4 | 3 500 – 4 500 |
| Side | 4 | 3 700 – 6 000 |
| Lara (Antalya) | 7 | 5 000 – 6 000 |
| Antalya center | 1 | 5 500 |

## Features

- Total price includes hotel + flights from Zurich
- Sort by price (ascending/descending) or rating
- Flight comparison (SunExpress, Pegasus, Edelweiss, Swiss)
- Clickable cards → Booking.com with pre-filled dates & guests
- Responsive design (mobile + desktop)

## Stack

Single `index.html` — no dependencies, no build step.
